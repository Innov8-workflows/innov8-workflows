What We Do

Lasting Repairs, Not Quick Fixes

A small roof problem rarely stays small. A single slipped tile lets water in, and before long you've got damp ceilings, rotten timbers and a much bigger bill. Storm Guard Roofing & Building carries out prompt, properly-done roof repairs across Dundee, Perth and Angus — so a minor issue never turns into a major one.

With over 10 years on the tools, we've seen every kind of roof problem the Scottish weather can throw up. We find the real cause, fix it properly, and back it with a written guarantee.

Roof Problems We Fix

  • Slipped, cracked or missing tiles & slates — replaced and re-secured.
  • Roof leaks & damp — traced to the real source and sealed.
  • Storm & wind damage — rapid, insurance-ready repairs.
  • Failed flashing & leadwork — around chimneys, valleys and abutments.
  • Loose or cracked ridge tiles — re-bedded and repointed.
  • Blocked or broken guttering causing overflow and damp walls.

Storm Damage & Emergencies

When a storm strips tiles or brings a branch down on your roof, you need someone fast. We prioritise emergency call-outs — making the roof safe and watertight first, then carrying out a permanent repair and providing photos and a report for your insurance if you need them.

Common Questions

Roof Repair FAQs

How much does a roof repair cost?

It depends on the problem — replacing a few slipped tiles is a small job, while tracing a hidden leak or repairing storm damage takes more time. We always give a clear, fixed quote after a free survey, so you know the price before any work starts.

Do you carry out emergency roof repairs?

Yes. Storm damage and leaks don't wait for office hours. Call us on 07950 814881 and we'll get out to make your roof safe and watertight as quickly as we can across Dundee, Perth and Angus.

Can you find the source of a leak?

Yes. Leaks often show up far from where the water is actually getting in. We investigate properly — checking tiles, flashing, valleys and the chimney — to find and fix the real cause, not just the symptom.

Is your work guaranteed?

Every repair is backed by a written workmanship guarantee, and we agree everything with you in writing before we start.
