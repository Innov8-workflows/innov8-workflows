Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Honest answers to the questions we hear most. For anything specific to your roof, we're always happy to take a look.

Do you offer free quotes?

Yes — all our quotes are free and no-obligation. Call, WhatsApp or fill in our form and we'll arrange a visit, often the same day. Sending a few photos of your roof helps us help you faster.

What areas do you cover?

We're based in the Dundee area and cover Dundee, Broughty Ferry, Monifieth, Perth, Scone, Errol, Forfar, Kirriemuir, Brechin, Arbroath and the wider Angus & Tayside region. See all the areas we cover.

Do you guarantee your work?

Yes. All our workmanship is backed by a written guarantee, and we agree everything with you in writing before we start a job.

How long have you been roofing?

Storm Guard has over 10 years' hands-on experience across domestic and commercial roofs throughout Dundee, Perth and Angus. More about us.

Do you carry out emergency or storm-damage repairs?

Yes. Storm damage and leaks don't wait for office hours — call us on 07950 814881 and we'll get out to make your roof safe and watertight as quickly as we can.

Do you work on both houses and commercial buildings?

Yes — we take on both domestic and commercial roofing and building work, from a single slipped slate to a full re-roof.

What kinds of roofs do you work with?

All of them — natural slate, clay and concrete tiles, leadwork, uPVC roofline and guttering, and traditional Scottish roofs. See our full range of services.

Can you help with a storm-damage insurance claim?

Yes. If your roof has been storm-damaged and you're claiming on your home insurance, we can take photos and provide a written report to support your claim.

Will you clean up after the work?

Always. We turn up when we say we will, keep you updated throughout, and leave your property clean and tidy when we're finished.

How do I get a quote or get in touch?

Call 07950 814881, use our freephone 0800 170 0310, WhatsApp us, or fill in the contact form. We'll get back to you the same day with honest advice.
