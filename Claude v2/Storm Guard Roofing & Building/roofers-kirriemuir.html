Roofing in Kirriemuir

Roofers Who Know Kirriemuir's Roofs

Storm Guard Roofing & Building are trusted local roofers working across Kirriemuir and out into the surrounding Angus Glens. Known as the Little Red Town for its characteristic local red sandstone, Kirriemuir has a wealth of traditional period properties — narrow historic streets lined with sandstone buildings, chimney stacks and natural slate roofs that have stood for well over a century. We know these roofs, and we know what it takes to keep them in good order.

As the gateway to Glen Clova, Glen Prosen and the wider Angus Glens, Kirriemuir also serves as a base for more remote hillside and glen properties that face some of the harshest upland weather in the region. We work right across this area, from the town centre out to the countryside beyond.

Period & Traditional Property Care

Kirriemuir's red sandstone buildings are a defining feature of the town, and they call for a considered approach. Traditional natural slate roofs on these properties need to be matched carefully, and repointing work must use breathable lime-based mortars rather than hard cement — which can trap moisture and cause long-term damage to the stonework. We're experienced in working on period and traditional buildings, and we take care to use the right materials and methods for the job.

Whether it's a slipped slate on a Victorian terrace, flashing repairs around a sandstone chimney stack, or a full re-roof on an older property, we approach each job with the attention it deserves. We understand that these buildings are worth protecting properly.

Upland Weather & Storm Response

Kirriemuir sits at the edge of the Angus uplands, and properties in and around the town — particularly those further into the glens — face weather that can be significantly harsher than in the lowlands. High winds, driving rain and heavy snow in winter put real strain on roofs, ridges and flashings. We regularly work on hillside and glen properties that have taken the brunt of a storm, and we respond quickly to get roofs watertight and safe again.

If you've had storm damage or spotted a problem after bad weather, don't leave it. A small repair now is far less costly than the damage a leak can cause over time. Call us on 07950 814881 and we'll get out to you as soon as we can.
