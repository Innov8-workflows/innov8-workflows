What We Do

Roof and Building Work, All in One Go

When you're already up at roof level, it makes sense to put the rest of the exterior right too. Storm Guard Roofing & Building isn't just a roofing company — we carry out the exterior building work that sits alongside a roof job, so you don't finish one thing and leave another half-done. It means a cleaner result, fewer trades on site and less hassle for you.

With over 10 years on the tools across Dundee, Perth and Angus, we know how Scottish weather attacks a building — not just at roof level, but in the walls, the render and the masonry below. We fix the lot, properly.

Exterior Building Work We Take On

  • Brickwork & stonework repairs — spalling, cracked or loose masonry made good.
  • Render & harling repairs — blown, cracked or missing sections patched and matched.
  • Exterior pointing — crumbling mortar raked out and repointed to stop water ingress.
  • Garden & boundary walls — repairs, rebuilds and coping stones reset.
  • Storm-damaged masonry — fallen chimney masonry, displaced render and wall sections rebuilt and made good.
  • General exterior maintenance — the small jobs that keep a property in good shape.

Roof and Building Work Together

For storm damage and insurance work in particular, having one team cover everything keeps things simple. We can assess the roof repairs and the masonry damage in a single visit, provide one quote and carry out the work in one go. There's no waiting for a second trade, no gap in the schedule and no finger-pointing about who is responsible for what — it's all on us.

If you're having a roof replaced and the parapet wall, chimney stack or render below needs attention, we can take care of that at the same time. See our new roofs page for more on full re-roof work.

Honest About Scope

We focus on exterior and building repairs that sit alongside roofing rather than full new-build extensions or large structural projects. If a job is better suited to a specialist builder, we'll tell you that honestly and point you in the right direction — we'd rather be straight with you than take on work we're not the best fit for.

Common Questions

General Building FAQs

What kind of building work do you do?

Alongside our roofing, we take on exterior building work — brickwork and stonework repairs, render and pointing, garden and boundary walls, and general exterior maintenance. It means we can finish a property off properly rather than leaving you to find another trade.

Can you do the roof and the building work in one job?

Yes, and it often makes sense to. For storm damage or insurance work especially, having one team handle the roof and the masonry keeps things simple.

Do you build extensions?

We focus on exterior and building repairs that complement our roofing work rather than full new-build extensions. If a job is better suited to a specialist, we'll tell you honestly.

Can you repair storm-damaged walls and masonry?

Yes. If a storm has brought down chimney masonry, render or part of a wall, we can rebuild and make good as part of the same visit.
